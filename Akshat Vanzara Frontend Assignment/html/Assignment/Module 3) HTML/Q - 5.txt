Q-5. What are different types of lists in HTML? With Example

* What are the types of lists in HTML?

lists can be used to show different pieces of information that are related to each other. Listing the features of a particular product can be a simple example of a list. So, lists are important for showing any information on websites, which is why they are available in HTML.

==>> There are three different types of list in HTML

1.Ordered list 
2.Unordered list 
3.Nested list  

* Example:

1.Ordered list :
                 <h1 style="margin-left: 2%;">Vegan Indian Street Foods List</h1>
    <hr style="margin-right: 63%; width: 35%;">
    <ol style="margin-left: 1%;">
        <li>Pani Puri</li>
        <li>Bhel Puri</li>
        <li>Sev Puri</li>
        <li>Samosa</li>
        <li>Aloo Tikki</li>
    </ol>


2.Unordered list :
                  <h2 style="margin-left: 2%;">Unorder List</h2>
    <hr style="margin-right: 63%; width: 35%;">

    <ul style="margin-left: 1%;">
        <li>Parantha</li>
        <li>Chhole Bhature</li>
        <li>Vada Pav</li>
        <li>Dabeli</li>
        <li>Pav Bhaji</li>
    </ul>
    

3.Nested list :
               <h1 style="margin-left: 2%;">Nested List</h1>
    <hr style="margin-right: 63%; width: 35%;">
     <ol>
         <li style="margin-left: 1%;"><b>IT Courses List</b></li>
         <br>
             <ul>
                <li>Computer Science</li>
                <li>Data Science</li>
                <li>Digital Marketing</li>
                <li>Artificial Intelligence</li>
                <li>Software Development</li>
             </ul>
             <br>
             <li style="margin-left: 1%;"><b>Non IT Courses List</b></li>
             <br>
                <ul>
                    <li>Business Analyst</li>
                    <li>Content Writer</li>
                    <li>Growth Marketing</li>
                    <li>Financial Analysis</li>
                </ul>
                
     </ol>
